Equipment

We manufacture a large range of drilling equipment for exploration purposes including pumps, winches, drill masts, and many other items.

Huts

Our modular Camp Huts can be flown into remote locations, providing high quality accommodation on location. We can supply a full package including all whiteware, bedding, and services.

We have also designed a compact heli-portable Rig Hut that has facilities for storage of equipment, outdoor gas cooker, and benches for completing essential paperwork.
