All children (ages 0-5) receive 30 free hours of care (so no cost to you).

Extra Hours

If a child does more than 6 hours per day at the centre then the hourly rate of $5.00 applies. Extra hours over the child’s booked times will also incur the $5.00 per hour charge.

WINZ Subsidy

All parents who qualify for a WINZ subsidy are expected to do so to offset our low fee structure. We will assist you with this.

Holidays and Absences

We do not charge for public holidays.

You will still be charged if your child is absent.

Every child is given 15 days holiday per year. If your child is away for longer than this please speak to the Centre Manager. Two week’s notice is required of any leave as it may affect staffing ratios.
