Fees (as of 1 March 2020)

Children 0-2 years old:   $1 per hour 

Children 3-5 years old:    30 hours free 

All children, all families, are eligible for the above rates up to a maximum of 6 hours per day. After that the regular rate of $5 per hour applies.

Casual Hours

If a child requires one off extra hours, or a one off extra day, the casual rate applies of $5 per hour 

Extra days or hours need to be in consultation with the centre manager, and will be granted if space is available.

WINZ Subsidies

If you think you may qualify for a WINZ subsidy and have a 0-2year old enrolling with us, we would appreciate you applying. Centre managers are able to help parents with filling in forms if needed.

Important to Note

  • We do not charge for public holidays,
  • We do charge if your child is absent for their booked times.
  • We may charge an Early/Late charge for any children unexpectedly dropped off prior to their booking, or picked up later than their booking. Late fees are $10 per event.
  • If management have been consulted and have granted the extra time (before or after your booking) you will be charged at the Casual Rate above, and will not incur the Early/Late charge.

Holidays

Every child may take 15 days holiday per year. When you have informed us that you are going on holiday, we will apply a 50% discount to your regular fees for the duration of your holiday. 

If your child is to be away for longer than 3 weeks please speak to the Centre Manager.

Please provide 2 weeks notice for holidays.
