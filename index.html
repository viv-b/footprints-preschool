Closest Preschool to Waihi Town Centre

Footprints Preschool is located near the heart of Waihi town. We are a purpose built centre with a large and varied natural outdoor environment.

Our centre consists of two rooms.

The 0-2 year olds area - Our Soul Room -Here your toddler can explore the world around them in a natural grassed area, and sandpit complete with water cascade, while your infant can experience plenty of new sights, smells, sounds and tastes safely inside and outside.  

The 2-5 year old area - The Heart Room - Here your preschooler can spend much of their day outdoors, exploring and creating in the sandpit, loose parts barkpit, mud kitchen, and mini stream. They will create masterpieces with art activities, or zoom around on the bikes. So bring plenty of changes of clothes, because learning is a messy business!
