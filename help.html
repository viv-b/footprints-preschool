Collection Ordering

When using a 'sort number', the higher number items in the collection will appear first. Applies to:

  • Services
  • Equipment Sales

With Projects, there is a 'date' field. This doesn't appear in the live site, but in the admin it can be seen on the summary page for the collection.

The ordering is from newest to oldest date.
